about us
About us:Nifty Clothing is a lifestyle brand that aims to fulfill men's and women's preferences in casual clothing at a reasonable price. Nifty Clothing offers a variety of styles that are suitable for every client we have. Our brand is committed to preserving and enhancing Filipino streetwear culture; it is designed to give comfort and convenience to clients.

At Nifty Clothing, we always keep an eye on the current trends while putting our customers' wishes first. Because of this, we have satisfied clients all over the world and are proud to be a part of the Nifty Clothing Industry.
Manila

The newest physical store of Nifty Clothing is in Manila, the center and the capital city of the Philippines, making it the ideal place to acquire exposure and promotion for our high-quality clothing.

Zambales

In early 2022, Nifty Clothing opened its first physical store designed to satisfy both digital and physical shopping in one location.

Nueva Ecija
The Zambales branch's success made it possible to open another physical store in Nueva Ecija. Nueva Ecijanos showed their love for Filipino culture streetwear; hence the Nueva Ecija branch has the most profitable sale in 2022.
